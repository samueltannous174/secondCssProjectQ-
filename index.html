Weather

Weather is the day-to-day or hour-to-hour change in the atmosphere. Weather includes wind, lightning, storms, hurricanes, tornadoes (also known as twisters), rain, hail, snow, and lots more. Energy from the Sun affects the weather too.

the season between autumn and spring comprising in the northern hemisphere usually the months of December, January, and February

Snow comprises individual ice crystals that grow while suspended in the atmosphere—usually within clouds—and then fall, accumulating on the ground where they undergo further changes.

Spring, also known as springtime, is one of the four temperate seasons, succeeding winter and preceding summer.
